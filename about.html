Precious Gabriel & Blowout

At the young age of 21, Precious Gabriel embarked on a venture that would soon revolutionize the indie game development scene. As the founder and CEO of Blowout, Precious has steered the company toward creating immersive and innovative gaming experiences that captivate audiences worldwide. With a strong foundation in data science, Precious has uniquely positioned Blowout at the intersection of technological advancement and creative storytelling.

Blowout isn't just a game development company; it's a testament to the power of combining analytical rigor with creative freedom. Under Precious's leadership, the company has launched several critically acclaimed titles, each reflecting a commitment to quality, player engagement, and pushing the boundaries of conventional gaming. As a bilingual entrepreneur fluent in English and Japanese, Precious's vision for Blowout transcends cultural and linguistic barriers, aiming to create games that resonate on a global scale.
